Experience the energy of Angeles City, Philippines in this virtual space!

An exhilarating world awaits you where real-life stores come alive through avatars.

Stroll down Fields Avenue and enter stores you're interested in with your avatar. Interact in real-time with actual store owners and user avatars. While you can only imagine the taste, the biggest thrill is feeling like you're actually in a physical store by hearing the live voices around you.

Moreover, with the decoration feature, you can freely design your store's space virtually. Even for those who found it difficult to open a physical store, you can now realize your whimsical space designs at a low cost in the virtual realm.

For a small monthly fee, you can establish your dream store. The points you earn can also be used to purchase actual products, allowing you to seamlessly go between the virtual and real worlds.

Escape your daily routine for an extraordinary Philippine experience. A new adventure awaits you there. Come on, log in and begin your unique virtual life!
